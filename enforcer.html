Enforcer

》Fire support:

The Enforcer is essential to teammates, providing useful cover fire which debilitates enemies.

》Special ability:

The Enforcer inflicts a much stronger, ADVANCED SUPPRESSION, on enemies.
It dazes enemies, making them unable to aim down sights and also slows them down.
The ability is PASSIVE and PERMANENT.
